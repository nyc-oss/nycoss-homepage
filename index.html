ABOUT CONTACT

New York Open Source Society (NYC OSS) is an organization designed to connect developers and techies of all varieties in New York City with the goal of furthering the world's greater open source codebase. We strive to help developers connect with other developers who can help them create useful and interesting software and other technologies. If you're interested, fill out your skill inventory here and we will reach out to you!
